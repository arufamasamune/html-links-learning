Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, thar appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the the main acrion or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
